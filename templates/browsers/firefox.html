Security Concerns on Android

Avoid Gecko-based browsers like Firefox on Android due to their vulnerabilities and lack of internal sandboxing.

Gecko-based browsers add a significant attack surface and bypass many hardening measures.

Firefox for Android does not have internal sandboxing, unlike Chromium-based browsers.

Detailed Analysis

Firefox's sandbox on Android is currently weak and lacks site isolation.

The desktop version's sandbox is gradually improving, but it's still weaker compared to other browsers, especially on Linux.

The Android version of Firefox does not utilize the OS isolatedProcess feature for strong isolation.
