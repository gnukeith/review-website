Firefox

Firefox is a free and open-source web browser developed by the Mozilla Foundation. It's known for its flexibility, performance, respect for privacy, and robust support for extensions.

Brave

Brave is a free and open-source web browser that's based on the Chromium web browser. The browser blocks ads and website trackers, and provides a way for users to send cryptocurrency contributions in the form of Basic Attention Tokens to websites and content creators.
